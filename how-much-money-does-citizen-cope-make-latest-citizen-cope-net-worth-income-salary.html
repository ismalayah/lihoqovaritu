Clarence Greenwood (better known by his stage name Citizen Cope) is an American composer, producer, and performer who lives in Los Angeles. His music is a mash-up of blues, soul, folk, and rock influences, among others. Ten years have passed since he began his professional touring career. Citizen Cope’s songs have been recorded by a wide range of musicians, including Carlos Santana, Dido, Pharoahe Monch, and Richie Havens, among others. In 2010, he established Rainwater Recordings after deciding that he no longer wanted to deal with big record companies. He now records and produces for his own record label, Rainwater Recordings. He has previously been signed to Capitol, Arista, DreamWorks, and RCA Records, among other companies.
