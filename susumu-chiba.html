Susumu Chiba

Susumu Chiba (Japanese: 千葉進歩 Chiba Susumu) is a Japanese voice actor for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Chiba was born on September 13, 1970 in Kanagawa, Japan, and became a voice actor as an adult. Chiba's most notable role on the Pokémon anime was that of Lance. He has also voiced a few Pokémon and characters of the day. Chiba was once affiliated with Marine Entertainment, and is now with Office Osawa.
