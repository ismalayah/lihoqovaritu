Are there different types of fill dirt?

The term “fill dirt” can describe many different types of dirt. Topsoil, subsoil, gravel, sand, and clay can all make up compactable fill in varying amounts to produce a product that is still considered “fill dirt.” The type of fill dirt used depends heavily on the project and its requirements.

How do you fill large low spots in lawn?
