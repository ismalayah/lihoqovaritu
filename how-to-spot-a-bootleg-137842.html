How to Spot A Bootleg | Page 4

What do you guys think of this website? Pokemon Figures, Toys, Cards, Plush Dolls & Gifts | PokemonZone.com

I think I might have found some bootleg times there before, but never bought them since they look fake like this one. Victini Black & White Pokemon Figure - Pokemon Dolls & Collectibles | PokemonZone.com When I saw it I knew right a way it's fake.

How is it fake?
1) Victini's body color is cream, the figure's body color is more yellow.
2) The hands and feet are done wrong and some of the body color's paint is on them as well.
3) The ears are suppose to look like the letter "V", the ears on the figure looks like they're curved outwards.
4) The hands, feet, and ears are colored the wrong shade of orange.
5) The inside of Vicitini's ears are black with with cream bordering it, the figure's ears lacked this feature.
6) The mouth was colored wrong and the teeth aren't colored in, it's also set too low on the face.
7) The eyes are colored incorrectly and are too small.
8) Tail wings are in the wrong position and shape.
9) No "V" mark on the body.
10) The part where the ears and head meet on the figure looks like that the ears could fall off at any moment when you pick up the figure or move it around at least.

I showed it to a friend who not really interested in Pokemon anymore and even he agreed that it's fake and that no one's going to buy it.
