Super Mario Crossover 2.0 Has Gone Live

Recommended Videos

Last month, we told you about Super Mario Crossover 2.0, the Flash game taking favorites of old and putting them into a pixel perfect replication of Super Mario Bros. Well, the update has finally gone live and it’s absolutely glorious. The characters all control just as you’d expect them to, and being able to play a pseudo Game Boy version of Bass running around world 1-1 is making the 80s kid in me squeel with absolute joy.

This is every lie you ever told in elementary school brought into the real world. Go check this out right now, I promise you won’t regret it.
