Brand New Concept Art For Pixars Monsters University

Recommended Videos

Pixar is starting to dish out marketing material for Monsters University and here at We Got This Covered, we couldn’t be happier. Yesterday we saw four near identical teasers and today we have two pieces of concept art. It looks as though Pixar is keeping a lot of plot details under wraps at the moment but sure enough they are building the hype train, slowly but surely.

If you’re unfamiliar with the film, check out the plot summary below.

“Mike Wazowski and James P. Sullivan are an inseparable pair, but that wasn’t always the case. From the moment these two mismatched monsters met they couldn’t stand each other. “Monsters University” unlocks the door to how Mike and Sully overcame their differences and became the best of friends.”

The first piece of art shows Mike and Sully strutting towards the titular academy. Not much can be gained from the photo but it does give off a very warm feeling, with the deep colour shading (coming from an art enthusiast). Be that as it may, we don’t get any hints towards a principle plot. The second picture exhibits the two crossing paths on the campus.

Unlike the first piece, we can take a bit more away from this one but still, it really doesn’t give us much to go on. One thing is for sure though, Pixar will not fail to show off the visual magic that they are known for.

Everything about the film looks great so far and I think it’s going to have no problem capitalizing on what made Monsters, Inc. so great.

Monsters University is enrolling in theatres on June 21, 2013.
