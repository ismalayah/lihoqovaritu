Does Carrie Ann Inaba have a baby girl or adopted one?

Carrie Ann Inaba is an American television personality, dancer, choreographer, actress, and singer. She is best known for her work on ABC TV’s Dancing with the Stars for which she has served as a judge since 2005.

Carrie Ann Inaba’s daughter: Does Carrie Ann Inaba have a baby girl or adopted one?

The TV personality has no children, but she has always wanted to be a mother. She has repeatedly stated that she is considering adoption. However, The 55-year-old is yet to fulfill her heart desires.
