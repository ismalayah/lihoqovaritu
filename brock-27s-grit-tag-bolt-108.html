Brock's Grit (Japanese: タケシのガッツ Takeshi's Guts) is a Supporter card. It was first released as part of the Evolutions expansion.

Card text

Shuffle 6 in any combination of Pokémon and basic Energy cards from your discard pile into your deck.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular card and a Full Art card in the Evolutions expansion, first released in the Japanese Expansion Pack 20th Anniversary subset. The Regular print features artwork by Ken Sugimori, while the Full Art print features artwork by Hitoshi Ariga. It was reprinted as both a Regular card and a Full Art card with new artwork in the Team Up expansion, first released in the Japanese Tag Bolt expansion. Both prints feature artwork by Naoki Saito. It was reprinted again in the Hidden Fates expansion with new artwork by Megumi Mizutani, first released in the Japanese Brock of Pewter City Gym Trainer Battle Deck.
